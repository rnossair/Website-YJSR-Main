What is Alternative Medicine?

Posted by Rohini Vummadi on August 1, 2022 Edited by Felea Joli Mago

Introduction

The term “Alternative medicine” was popularized during the 1970s and marketed as a wide range of treatments that can be used instead of conventional or standard treatments.

Alternative medicine may include diets, herbs, and even teas, and is usually supported by a culture's tradition.

The 5 categories of alternative medicine:

● Biological-based treatments

- incorporate some natural resources.

● Alternative medical systems

- focuses on health theories such as Chinese and Ayurvedic medicine

● Energy therapies

- utilizes energy sources or fields to influence healing.

● Mind - body interventions

- assists people in healing through relaxation techniques such as yoga and tai chi.

● Manipulative and body - based methods

-tries to manipulate the body to treat certain conditions.

Is the term “alternative medicine” interchangeable?

Well, to know the answer -we must learn four common definitions.:

❖ Complementary medicine - “A variety of medical therapies that are outside the scope of scientific medicine but may be used in combination with the others to treat disease and illness.” It is also known to relieve pain, increase relaxation, and provide numerous other health benefits.

- Examples: Acupuncture and Osteopathy

❖ Holistic medicine - “Refers to treatment that considers a person's overall health rather than choosing to focus only on one organ or bodily system. Some complementary, integrative, and alternative medicine practices are holistic.

❖ Integrative medicine - “A form of medical therapy that combines practices and treatments from alternative medicine with conventional medicine.” It is also referred to as a partnership between both the person and the professional in charge.

❖ Alternative medicine - Another treatment practice or system that can be used in place of the standard.

➢ These four terms are frequently used interchangeably, but keep in mind that they each have a distinct meaning.

Which alternative medicine is the best?

Although not mentioned in the above category, homeopathy is one of the most natural, and accepted forms of medicine since it is made out of natural resources like plants.

Aside from that, it is also safe for everyone to use, which includes pregnant women, newborns, and children. Making it a popular choice for people to get treated or treat others in this way.

Some background information:

1) Researchers discovered that homeopathy is the longest complementary and alternative medicine (CAM) to have emerged in Europe.

2) It was founded by Samuel Hahnemann, a well respected physician in Germany.

3) Was developed almost 200 years ago, so all-in all, pretty accurate.

4) Linked to two theories:

"like cures like”

- the concept that a disease can be cured by a substance that causes similar effects in normal individuals

"law of minimum dose"

- the assumption that the lower the dose of medication, the more efficient it is.

3 main reasons why alternative medicine is favored compared to conventional medicine:

1) Dissatisfied with conventional treatments.

2) They see alternative treatments as offering more personal autonomy and control over health care decisions (J.Astin, 1998).

3) The alternatives are seen as more compatible with the patients' values, worldview, or beliefs regarding the nature and meaning of health and illness (J.Astin, 1998).

Pros and cons of using alternative medicines:

Pros:

★ Whole-person treatments are available

★ Personal attention

★ Focus on prevention

Cons

● Limited scientific research - although discovered 200 years ago, not supported by scientific research

● Confusing marketing

● Potentially dangerous interactions

Conclusion:

In general, alternative medicine is just another form of getting treatment. IF conventional medicine is not the right fit for you nor your health - maybe alternative medicine is.

It all comes down to personal preference and professional advice. Each form of medicine has its own advantages and disadvantages, we just have to look for the perfect fit in the right direction
